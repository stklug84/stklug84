### Hi there 👋, I'm Steffen

PhD physicist who loves to design, build and deliver production-grade
distributed data & analytics platforms on Apache **Spark / Kafka / Flink**
(PySpark, Scala, Python) and cloud-native **AI/ML** systems. I act as a trusted
technical advisor to cross-functional teams across Europe, North America and
Asia, bringing **MLOps** and **CI/CD** discipline to productionise analytics at
scale. My hands-on strengths in **GenAI** — RAG, multi-agent systems and
evaluation — sit alongside semantic data, explainable AI and knowledge graphs,
so I can ship agentic solutions that deliver measurable business value.

### 🔭 What I'm working on

I'm currently architecting an end-to-end Data & AI platform on a **lakehouse**
spanning hybrid and public cloud, delivering production-grade data engineering
and analytics in an industrial, manufacturing context.

- 🤖 Rich-code **multi-agent orchestration** platforms (LangGraph, Google ADK) —
  autonomous, dynamic agentic systems that adapt their topology to the task at hand.
- 🏗️ End-to-end **lakehouse platform architecture** across hybrid and public cloud.
- 🌊 **Near-real-time streaming** pipelines (Kafka, Flink, Delta Lake) powering
  early-warning and anomaly-detection systems.
- 🚀 **MLOps at scale** — from data pipelines through model training, deployment,
  drift detection and automated retraining on edge and cloud.
